About ME

 I am a Chinese student studying in Japan at the Faculty of Frontier Science of Konan University(2022). Here, I will share my learning, life and interests, and hope to communicate and interact with you.
I have been interested in natural science since I was a child, especially chemistry and biology. I think nature is very wonderful, and I want to learn natural science knowledge mainly based on chemistry and biology, explain some biological mechanisms by chemistry, and then apply them in other fields, such as pharmacy or materials science.

 The study life in this faculty is much harder than I imagined. Starting from the first year, we have to do experiments from one o’clock in the afternoon until seven or eight o’clock in the evening every Tuesday. In the second year, even two days have to do experiments like this, but I am personally very interested in experiments and research, and enjoy it.
 

 In this blog, I will record all aspects of my experiments or research, including experimental design, experimental process, experimental results, experimental analysis, experimental experience and so on. I will also share some interesting natural science knowledge that I learned in class or in extracurricular reading, hoping to bring you some inspiration and gains.
 

 In addition to natural science, I also have other hobbies. I am very interested in Japanese ACG culture. I watched a lot of Japanese anime before I came to Japan, so I might also write some content about anime, manga or light novels in this blog, share my impressions, recommendations, comments and so on. I am also keen on photography, so I will also post some photos I took here to show my perspective and feelings.
 

 My high school life was spent in Japan. I went to Japan after finishing the second year of high school in China. I went to Kyoto Ryoyo High School(2021) for the third year of high school. The one year of high school life in Japan is my most unforgettable memory so far. Although I didn’t join any clubs at that time because I was in the third year of high school, I still met many good friends and experienced many interesting things. In this blog, I will also recall some of my high school time and share my stories and thoughts with you.
 

 In short, this blog is my personal space to record my growth and experience. If you are interested in my content, please follow my blog and leave your comments and suggestions.
